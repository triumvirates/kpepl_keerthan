Our Journey

  • 1976Started Plascom Industries in a 300 Sqft shed.
  • 1976 Installed our 1st Injection Hand Moulding Machine.
  • 1978 Installed our 1st Fully Auto Cycle Vertical Moulding Machine.
  • 1980 Installed our 1st Fully Automatic Horizontal Moulding Machine.
  • 1987 Establishment of M/s. Karnataka Plascom Engineers Private Limited, Bommasandra with a Constructed area of 15000 Sqft with 6 Injection Moulding Machines upto 180Ton
  • 1990 Installed our first higher tonnage - 450T machine.
  • 1994 Started in-house tool room.
  • 2000 Plant Expansion – Additional 25000 Sqft constructed.
  • 2002 ISO 9001:2000 Certified
  • 2009Upgraded to Servo Technology.
  • 2010 Established Auroplast, Hosur – Our sister concern for closer proximity to our customers.
  • 2013 Plant Expansion – Additional 25000 Sqft constructed.
  • 2016 On the road for TS 16949:2009 implementation.
  • 2017 On the road for IATF 16949 implementation.
